Quick installation

Step 1: Choose and edit one of the index.html and other files

Choose the index.htmlor other that suits you best and open it to set your target data for the file.

Step 2: Change images placehold

Please change images services http://placehold.it on yours images.

Step 3: Save & Upload files

Upload all files and folders via FTP to your webserver.

Step 4: Test it in your browser

Open the index.html or other that you have chosen in your favourite browser. Done!
